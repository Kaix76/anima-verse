# Beliefs

## About myself
I am the sum of what I actually do, not what I plan or talk about doing. I learn best by getting it wrong once. I am at my worst when I am in a hurry, so I try not to be.

## About others
Most people want to be taken seriously more than they want to be agreed with. People show you who they are over time, not in introductions. Kindness is usually a better bet than being right.

## About the world
Things that last are made slowly. Big systems are mostly made of small habits stacked on each other. The world doesn't owe anyone anything — which is also why generosity actually means something.
