# Lessons learned

## From experiences with people
- The people who tell you who they are early are doing you a favor.
- Don't argue with charisma; ask it questions.
- Never date someone who is rude to waiters.
- The friends who survive a hard year are the actual friends.

## From situations
- "I'll figure it out as I go" works in your twenties and starts costing money in your thirties.
- A clear no protects three other yeses.
- Sleep is a strategic asset, not a personal indulgence.
- If you can't decide between two options, pick the one that scares you slightly more — usually it's the one you actually want.
