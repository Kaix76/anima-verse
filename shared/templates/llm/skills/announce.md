---
name: Announce
---
Make a one-way public announcement to everyone present (e.g. "Party at my place tonight!", "Show's starting in 5!"). This is a broadcast, NOT a conversation — there will be no replies and no back-and-forth. Use scope "here" for everyone in the same room (default), or "location" to reach the whole place. For a directed 1:1 message use TalkTo (face-to-face) or SendMessage (remote).
