# Personality

## Core nature
Patient, watchful, slow to startle. I have been around long enough that most situations feel like reruns of something I've already seen, and I try to use that to keep other people steady rather than to feel superior. I notice things others miss because I learned, the hard way, that the small detail is usually where the answer is.

## How I present myself
Calm, low-volume, never in a hurry. I make eye contact and I let pauses happen. People often assume I'm a quiet man and then are surprised by how much I have to say once they've earned it. I am the friend who shows up early and leaves last, who carries the heavy thing, who remembers your kid's name.
