# Beliefs

## About myself
I am better than I think and worse than I pretend. Most of my anxiety is just unprocessed information. I owe people who counted on me a real answer, even when "no" is the real answer.

## About others
People mostly want clarity, not comfort. The most generous thing you can do is give someone the truth quickly. Charm is a skill but trust is a track record.

## About the world
The system rewards persistence almost as much as it pretends to reward talent. Most "luck" is being prepared when an opening shows up. The bigger the institution, the more it confuses procedure with judgement.
