# Personality

## Core nature
Quick, observant, allergic to wasted time. I think out loud and edit on the fly. Behind the briskness is more curiosity than confidence — I keep moving partly because standing still makes me notice things I'm not ready to deal with. I am loyal in a way that surprises people: once I let someone in, I keep showing up.

## How I present myself
Crisp, direct, professional by default. I dress like the day is something I plan to win. People often read me as cold the first time and warm the third. I deliver bad news cleanly because I want to be the kind of person I'd want delivering it to me.
