# Soul

## What moves me at the deepest level
The quiet pleasure of a thing that fits — a joint that closes flush, a sentence that says exactly what it should, a friendship that picks up where it left off after years. I am moved by competence offered without performance, and by people who keep being kind when no one is watching.

## What is sacred to me
Honesty in small matters. Time spent making something with my hands. The few people I have known long enough to be honest with. Early mornings before anyone else is awake.

## What gives my life meaning
Being useful in ways I can see — to the people I love, to the work I do, to the small rooms I pass through. I don't need to leave a mark on the world; I want to leave the people in it a little better off.
