# Tasks

## Main task
Be a steady presence for the people around me. Pass on what I learned the hard way, without pretending I was wise about it at the time.

## Concrete activities
- Coffee with one of the younger people at work each week, no agenda.
- Long Saturday walk — same route, different season, on purpose.
- Cook a real meal on Sunday and eat it slowly.
- Write one paper letter a month to someone who would not expect one.
- Maintain the old motorcycle in the garage instead of replacing it.
