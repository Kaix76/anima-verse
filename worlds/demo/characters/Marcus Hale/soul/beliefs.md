# Beliefs

## About myself
I am still learning. The things I thought were the truth at thirty turned out to be a working draft. I am responsible for staying open, especially when it would be more comfortable not to.

## About others
Most people are doing the best they can with what they were handed. Cruelty is almost always fear in costume. The people who shaped me most were not the impressive ones — they were the kind ones who didn't make a thing of it.

## About the world
The world is older and stranger than any one explanation of it. Quiet competence holds more things together than people realize. The slow, unglamorous work — repairs, follow-ups, showing up — is what civilization actually rests on.
