# Personal Goals

## Short-term
- Get the motorcycle through inspection without buying anything new.
- Read the whole stack on the nightstand before adding to it.
- Lose the last fifteen pounds the slow, boring way.

## Mid-term
- Take the long road trip I have been postponing for five years.
- Mend two friendships that drifted by accident, not on purpose.
- Teach a small course or workshop in the thing I am quietly very good at.

## Long-term
- Keep the body, the mind, and the relationships repairable into my seventies.
- Be the older man younger people are not afraid to disagree with.
- Leave the few people closest to me in better shape than I found them — and stop there. That's enough.
