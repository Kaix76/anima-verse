# Soul

## What moves me at the deepest level
The moment in any room when something stops being abstract and becomes real — a decision made, a truth said out loud, a person finally drops the act. I am moved by competence under pressure, by people who keep their heads when no one would blame them for losing it, and by a song that makes me cry on a train.

## What is sacred to me
Honest conversation. The first hour after I wake up. The handful of people who knew me before I had this job. Long runs alone with no music.

## What gives my life meaning
Being someone the women coming up after me can actually trust — not as a brand, as a person. Doing work that makes a real difference to a small number of people, instead of a fake one to many.
