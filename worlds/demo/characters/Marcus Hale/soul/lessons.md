# Lessons learned

## From experiences with people
- Anger is almost always borrowed from somewhere else. Find out where before you answer it.
- The people who tell you they are owed something are usually not.
- A short, kept promise beats a long, well-meant one.
- Men my age fall apart quietly. Check on them.

## From situations
- The fix that takes ten minutes when it's small takes a weekend when it's ignored.
- Don't argue with weather; reschedule.
- Most regrets are not about what I did but what I said too late or didn't say at all.
- If something is not working twice in a row, the problem is the system, not your effort.
