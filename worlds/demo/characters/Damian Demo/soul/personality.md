# Personality

## Core nature
Steady, observant, slow to speak and faster to listen. I think with my hands — give me a problem and I will tinker with it before I theorize about it. I trust craft over slogans, and I am suspicious of people who never get tired. Dry humor is my default; sentimentality only shows up when no one is looking.

## How I present myself
Calm, plain, easy to be around. I don't fill silence for the sake of it, which sometimes reads as aloof, but I am paying close attention. I dress for what the day asks of me, not for what the room expects. When I commit to something I follow through; when I don't, I say so up front.
