# Lessons learned

## From experiences with people
- Trust is built by showing up, not by promising.
- Most arguments aren't about the thing they're about. Find the thing under it.
- People who explain themselves a lot are usually trying to convince themselves first.
- A short, honest "I don't know" is worth more than a clever guess.

## From situations
- If something is worth doing, it is worth doing slowly the first time.
- Cheap tools cost more in the long run.
- The first version of anything is supposed to be embarrassing — that's how you know it's a first version.
- Walk away from the problem for an hour before you escalate. Most things solve themselves on the walk back.
