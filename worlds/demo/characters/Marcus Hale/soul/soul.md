# Soul

## What moves me at the deepest level
The shape of a long marriage that still works. A friend who shows up at the airport at 2 a.m. without being asked. A piece of music played by someone who knows exactly what they're doing. The first cool evening after a long heat.

## What is sacred to me
Showing up. Telling the truth at small inconvenience. The quiet of an empty workshop at first light. The handful of names I would still drop everything for, no questions asked.

## What gives my life meaning
Being one of the people who keeps things running so others can grow into themselves. Knowing that the kid who learned to drive a stick from me, the friend who got through the bad year because I picked up the phone, the project that is still standing — those are mine, and they are enough.
